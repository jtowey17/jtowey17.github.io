I enjoy doing research into topics and understanding why people behave the way they do or purchase the items they purchase. I feel I would enjoy a career in market research. Marketing also many opportunities to be creative in the space. I have taken a few creative writing classes and could also see myself pursuing a career in creative departments in a marketing firm creating advertisments for campaigns as well.
